Taylor Rig & Equipment LLC (USA) Industrial Engineering Internship Project Jan 2026 – Present

Delivered a production flow and materials planning improvement that reduced end-to-end lead time from 45 days to 30 days.

Scope included WIP flow control, line capacity utilization, workstation balancing, and supplier sequencing. The implementation stabilized execution, improved schedule adherence, and increased throughput while improving working-capital efficiency through demand-driven purchasing.
